My Coding Journey

By Rahul Bhagat | August 15 , 2023

I started coding in my first year of college, beginning with C and HTML. Over time, I’ve explored JS, React, and backend technologies like Node.js and MongoDB.

Here’s what I’ve learned along the way:

  • Consistency matters more than speed.
  • Debugging teaches patience.
  • Building projects is the best way to learn.
